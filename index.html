WTF Warthog?

Warthog is an experimental cryptocurrency written for fun by a group of crypto enthusiasts in their free time. This project is not meant to be serious but everyone is welcome to join and contribute. Who knows what this project will grow into?

Tokenomics

This is not the default kind of project where you see a disproportionately high allocation of the total supply unfairly reserved for the team, the advisors and their pockets. 0% is minted, premined, reserved for anyone, 100% is publicly mineable.

Remember the time when crypto was interesting and fun? We want to bring this golden age of crypto back to life.
